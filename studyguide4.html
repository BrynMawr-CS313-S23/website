Sample Questions

  • Suppose we are ray tracing a 400x300 image. The camera is at location (0,0,2) and the image plane is centered at (0,0,0) with minimum corner at (-2,-1.5) and maximum corner at (2, 1.5). What is the camera ray at pixel (100, 100)?

  • Suppose we have a ray at position (0,0,2) and direction (0,0,-1). At what point would it intersect the sphere centered at (1,0,0) with radius (10)?

  • Suppose we have a ray at position (0,0,2) and direction (0,0,-1). At what point would it intersect the box centered at (0,0,0) with dimensions (3,1,1)?

  • Suppose we have a ray at position (0,0,2) and direction (0,0,-1). At what point would it intersect the triangle with corners (-1,-1,0), (1,-1,0), (0,4,0)?
